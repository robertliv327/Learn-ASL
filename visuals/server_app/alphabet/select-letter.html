Practice a Letter of Your Choice

This challenge is designed to test your ability to recall a hand sign from memory rather than your ability to replicate a sign from an image.

To begin, decide which letter you want to practice and place one hand over the Leap Motion Device to see it displayed on the screen.

Next, attempt to make the correct sign and run a test by pressing the letter that you chose on the keyboard.

Feel free to perform multiple trials of different letters!
